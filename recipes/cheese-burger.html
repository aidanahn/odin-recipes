Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cook your ground beef and season
  2. Put the cheese on and melt
  3. Toast your buns
  4. Place vegetables on buns first
  5. Place patty on top
  6. Put bun on top
  7. Enjoy!
