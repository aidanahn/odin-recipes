French Fries

Description

Delight in the classic comfort of golden crispy fries, perfectly seasoned and fried to a crunchy exterior with a soft, fluffy interior. These fries are made from fresh, hand-cut potatoes and are the perfect side dish or snack, offering a satisfying balance of salty and savory flavors. Ideal for dipping in ketchup, mayonnaise, or your favorite sauce, these fries are a crowd-pleaser that never goes out of style.

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into fry shape
  2. Heat up vegetable oil
  3. Cook potatoes until crunchy
  4. Put salt to taste after taking out
  5. Enjoy!
